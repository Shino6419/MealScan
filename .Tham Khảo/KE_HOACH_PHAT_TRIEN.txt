KE HOACH PHAT TRIEN APP MEALSCAN

Mo ta tong quan
- MealScan la app React Native dung AI de nhan dien mon an tu hinh anh.
- Sau khi nhan dien, app du doan calo va thong tin dinh duong co ban.
- Nguoi dung co the them bua an vao lich su an uong.
- App thong ke tong calo da tieu thu theo tung ngay, giup nguoi dung theo doi thoi quen an uong.


1. CHUC NANG TAI KHOAN

1.1. Man hinh Dang nhap
- Nhap email hoac ten dang nhap.
- Nhap mat khau.
- Nut Dang nhap.
- Link chuyen sang Dang ky.
- Link quen mat khau neu can.
- Hien thi loi khi email/mat khau sai.
- Luu trang thai dang nhap de lan sau mo app khong can dang nhap lai.

1.2. Man hinh Dang ky
- Nhap ho ten.
- Nhap email.
- Nhap mat khau.
- Nhap lai mat khau.
- Nut Tao tai khoan.
- Kiem tra email hop le.
- Kiem tra mat khau du dai toi thieu.
- Hien thi loi neu email da ton tai hoac mat khau khong khop.
- Sau khi dang ky thanh cong co the tu dong dang nhap hoac chuyen ve man hinh Dang nhap.

1.3. Man hinh Quen mat khau
- Nhap email.
- Gui yeu cau dat lai mat khau.
- Hien thi thong bao sau khi gui thanh cong.


2. THANH NAVIGATION DUOI

Sau khi dang nhap thanh cong, app hien thi Bottom Tab Navigation gom 3 nut:

2.1. Thong ke
- Icon goi y: bar chart, home, calendar.
- Chuyen den man hinh thong ke chinh.

2.2. Chup anh
- Icon goi y: camera, scan.
- Chuyen den man hinh chup anh/nhan dien mon an.

2.3. Profile/Setting
- Icon goi y: user, settings.
- Chuyen den man hinh ho so va cai dat.


3. CAC MAN HINH CHINH CAN LAM

3.1. Man hinh Thong ke chinh
Muc dich:
- La man hinh tong quan ve luong calo nguoi dung da tieu thu.

Chuc nang can co:
- Hien thi tong calo da an trong ngay hien tai.
- Hien thi muc tieu calo moi ngay cua nguoi dung.
- Hien thi calo con lai hoac calo vuot muc tieu.
- Bieu do thong ke calo theo ngay trong tuan.
- Danh sach cac bua an trong ngay.
- Moi item bua an gom:
  + Hinh anh mon an.
  + Ten mon an.
  + So calo uoc tinh.
  + Thoi gian an.
- Loc/thay doi ngay de xem lich su cac ngay truoc.
- Nut xem chi tiet mot bua an.
- Nut xoa bua an khoi lich su neu nguoi dung nhap sai.

Du lieu can luu:
- Tong calo theo ngay.
- Danh sach bua an.
- Thoi gian them bua an.
- Anh mon an.
- Ket qua AI nhan dien.


3.2. Man hinh Chup anh
Muc dich:
- Cho phep nguoi dung chup anh mon an hoac chon anh tu thu vien de AI nhan dien.

Chuc nang can co:
- Mo camera.
- Xin quyen truy cap camera.
- Chup anh mon an.
- Chon anh tu thu vien.
- Xem lai anh truoc khi gui AI.
- Nut Phan tich mon an.
- Trang thai loading khi AI dang xu ly.
- Hien thi loi neu khong nhan dien duoc mon an.

Sau khi AI phan tich xong:
- Chuyen sang man hinh Ket qua nhan dien hoac hien thi modal ket qua.


3.3. Man hinh Ket qua nhan dien mon an
Muc dich:
- Cho nguoi dung xem, sua va xac nhan ket qua truoc khi them vao lich su.

Chuc nang can co:
- Hien thi anh mon an vua chup/chon.
- Hien thi ten mon an AI nhan dien.
- Hien thi calo uoc tinh.
- Hien thi thong tin dinh duong neu co:
  + Protein.
  + Carbs.
  + Fat.
  + Khau phan/serving size.
- Cho phep nguoi dung sua ten mon an.
- Cho phep nguoi dung sua calo neu AI du doan sai.
- Cho phep chon loai bua an:
  + Sang.
  + Trua.
  + Toi.
  + An nhe.
- Nut Them vao lich su.
- Nut Chup lai/Chon anh khac.

Du lieu can gui vao lich su:
- User ID.
- Anh mon an.
- Ten mon an.
- Calo.
- Nutrition info.
- Loai bua an.
- Ngay gio tao.


3.4. Man hinh Lich su bua an
Muc dich:
- Quan ly tat ca bua an da them.

Chuc nang can co:
- Hien thi danh sach bua an theo ngay.
- Loc theo ngay/tuan/thang.
- Tim kiem mon an theo ten.
- Xem chi tiet bua an.
- Sua thong tin bua an.
- Xoa bua an.
- Tu dong cap nhat lai thong ke sau khi sua/xoa.

Ghi chu:
- Man hinh nay co the nam trong tab Thong ke, khong nhat thiet phai la mot tab rieng.


3.5. Man hinh Chi tiet bua an
Muc dich:
- Hien thi chi tiet mot mon an da luu.

Chuc nang can co:
- Hinh anh mon an.
- Ten mon an.
- Calo.
- Protein, carbs, fat neu co.
- Loai bua an.
- Ngay gio da them.
- Nut Sua.
- Nut Xoa.


3.6. Man hinh Profile/Setting
Muc dich:
- Quan ly thong tin nguoi dung va cai dat app.

Chuc nang can co:
- Hien thi anh dai dien.
- Hien thi ten nguoi dung.
- Hien thi email.
- Sua thong tin ca nhan.
- Cai dat muc tieu calo moi ngay.
- Cai dat thong tin co the de tinh toan tot hon:
  + Tuoi.
  + Gioi tinh.
  + Chieu cao.
  + Can nang.
  + Muc do van dong.
  + Muc tieu: giam can, giu can, tang can.
- Doi mat khau.
- Dang xuat.
- Xoa tai khoan neu can.


4. CAC CHUC NANG BACKEND / DU LIEU CAN CO

4.1. Authentication
- Dang ky tai khoan.
- Dang nhap.
- Dang xuat.
- Luu session/token.
- Bao ve cac man hinh chinh, chi cho vao khi da dang nhap.

4.2. User Profile
- Luu thong tin nguoi dung.
- Luu muc tieu calo moi ngay.
- Cap nhat profile.

4.3. AI Food Recognition
- Gui anh mon an len AI.
- Nhan ve ten mon an.
- Nhan ve calo uoc tinh.
- Nhan ve nutrition info neu AI ho tro.
- Xu ly truong hop AI tra ve ket qua khong chac chan.

4.4. Meal History
- Them bua an moi.
- Lay danh sach bua an theo user.
- Lay bua an theo ngay.
- Sua bua an.
- Xoa bua an.

4.5. Statistics
- Tinh tong calo theo ngay.
- Tinh tong calo theo tuan.
- Tinh trung binh calo moi ngay.
- So sanh voi muc tieu calo.
- Tao du lieu cho bieu do.


5. GOI Y CAU TRUC NAVIGATION

Luon di theo flow:

- Neu chua dang nhap:
  + Dang nhap
  + Dang ky
  + Quen mat khau

- Neu da dang nhap:
  + Bottom Tab Navigation
    + Tab Thong ke
      - Man hinh Thong ke chinh
      - Man hinh Lich su bua an
      - Man hinh Chi tiet bua an
    + Tab Chup anh
      - Man hinh Chup anh
      - Man hinh Ket qua nhan dien mon an
    + Tab Profile/Setting
      - Man hinh Profile/Setting
      - Man hinh Sua profile
      - Man hinh Cai dat muc tieu calo


6. THU TU UU TIEN NEN LAM

Giai doan 1: Nen tang app
- Tao navigation.
- Tao man hinh Dang nhap.
- Tao man hinh Dang ky.
- Tao Bottom Tab Navigation voi 3 tab.
- Tao UI co ban cho Thong ke, Chup anh, Profile.

Giai doan 2: Xu ly du lieu co ban
- Tao model du lieu cho user.
- Tao model du lieu cho meal history.
- Them bua an thu cong de test truoc khi tich hop AI.
- Hien thi lich su bua an.
- Tinh tong calo theo ngay.

Giai doan 3: Tich hop camera va AI
- Xin quyen camera.
- Chup anh/chon anh tu thu vien.
- Gui anh len AI.
- Nhan ket qua mon an va calo.
- Cho phep nguoi dung sua ket qua.
- Them vao lich su.

Giai doan 4: Thong ke va hoan thien UX
- Ve bieu do calo theo ngay/tuan.
- Hien thi muc tieu calo va calo con lai.
- Them filter theo ngay/tuan/thang.
- Hoan thien profile va setting.
- Xu ly loading, empty state, error state.

Giai doan 5: Hoan thien truoc khi demo
- Kiem tra luong dang nhap/dang ky.
- Kiem tra luong chup anh -> AI -> them vao lich su.
- Kiem tra thong ke co cap nhat dung sau khi them/sua/xoa bua an.
- Lam UI gon gang, de dung.
- Test tren thiet bi that neu co.


7. CAC MAN HINH TOI THIEU CHO BAN DEMO

- Dang nhap.
- Dang ky.
- Thong ke chinh.
- Chup anh.
- Ket qua nhan dien mon an.
- Profile/Setting.

Neu muon demo nhanh, co the uu tien:
- Dang nhap/dang ky co ban.
- Chup anh/chon anh.
- AI tra ve ten mon an va calo.
- Them vao lich su.
- Thong ke tong calo theo ngay.
