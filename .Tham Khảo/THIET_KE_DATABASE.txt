THIET KE DATABASE CHO DU AN MEALSCAN

Phien ban hien tai:
- App chi tap trung vao viec nguoi dung nhap muc tieu calo can tieu thu moi ngay.
- AI nhan dien mon an va du doan so calo.
- Nguoi dung xac nhan mon an, sau do app luu vao lich su.
- Man hinh thong ke hien thi tong calo da tieu thu theo tung ngay va so sanh voi muc tieu calo moi ngay.

Cong nghe de xuat:
- Database: Supabase PostgreSQL.
- Authentication: Supabase Auth.
- Storage: Supabase Storage de luu anh mon an va avatar.


1. TONG QUAN CAC BANG

1.1. auth.users
- Bang co san cua Supabase Auth.
- Luu tai khoan dang nhap/dang ky.
- Khong can tu tao bang nay.

1.2. profiles
- Luu thong tin co ban cua user.
- Luu muc tieu calo can tieu thu moi ngay.
- Moi user trong auth.users co 1 dong trong profiles.

1.3. meals
- Luu cac mon an/bua an da duoc nguoi dung them vao lich su.
- Day la bang du lieu chinh de tinh tong calo theo ngay.

1.4. meal_ai_results
- Luu ket qua AI nhan dien mon an tu anh.
- Dung de hien thi ket qua truoc khi nguoi dung bam Them vao lich su.

1.5. daily_summaries
- Luu tong calo theo tung ngay.
- Giup man hinh Thong ke load nhanh.


2. CHI TIET CAC BANG

2.1. profiles

Muc dich:
- Luu thong tin tai khoan hien thi trong Profile/Setting.
- Luu muc tieu calo moi ngay do nguoi dung tu nhap.

Cot du lieu:
- id: uuid, khoa chinh, lien ket auth.users.id.
- full_name: text, ten nguoi dung.
- avatar_url: text, duong dan anh dai dien.
- daily_calorie_goal: integer, muc tieu calo moi ngay.
- timezone: text, mui gio cua user. Mac dinh Asia/Ho_Chi_Minh.
- created_at: timestamptz, thoi gian tao.
- updated_at: timestamptz, thoi gian cap nhat.

Khong can luu trong phien ban hien tai:
- tuoi.
- gioi tinh.
- chieu cao.
- can nang.
- muc do van dong.
- muc tieu giam can/tang can.


2.2. meals

Muc dich:
- Luu mon an da duoc nguoi dung xac nhan them vao lich su.

Cot du lieu:
- id: uuid, khoa chinh.
- user_id: uuid, user so huu mon an.
- meal_date: date, ngay an, dung de thong ke theo ngay.
- eaten_at: timestamptz, thoi diem an hoac thoi diem them.
- meal_type: text, loai bua an. Gia tri: breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack.
- food_name: text, ten mon an.
- calories: integer, so calo cua mon an.
- image_url: text, anh mon an luu tren Supabase Storage.
- source: text, nguon tao du lieu. Gia tri: ai_scan, manual.
- notes: text, ghi chu tuy chon.
- created_at: timestamptz, thoi gian tao.
- updated_at: timestamptz, thoi gian cap nhat.

Ghi chu:
- Bang nay khong luu protein, carbs, fat vi app hien tai chi quan tam calo.


2.3. meal_ai_results

Muc dich:
- Luu ket qua AI tra ve khi phan tich anh mon an.
- Sau khi nguoi dung sua/xac nhan, ket qua duoc them vao bang meals.

Cot du lieu:
- id: uuid, khoa chinh.
- user_id: uuid, user gui anh.
- meal_id: uuid, lien ket voi meals neu da them vao lich su.
- image_url: text, anh da gui AI.
- detected_food_name: text, ten mon AI nhan dien.
- estimated_calories: integer, calo AI du doan.
- confidence: numeric, do tin cay 0 den 1 neu AI co tra ve.
- raw_response: jsonb, ket qua goc tu AI.
- status: text, trang thai. Gia tri: pending, success, failed, confirmed, rejected.
- error_message: text, loi neu AI that bai.
- created_at: timestamptz, thoi gian tao.


2.4. daily_summaries

Muc dich:
- Luu tong calo theo ngay cua tung user.
- Dung cho man hinh thong ke chinh.

Cot du lieu:
- id: uuid, khoa chinh.
- user_id: uuid.
- summary_date: date.
- total_calories: integer, tong calo da tieu thu trong ngay.
- meal_count: integer, so mon/bua an da them trong ngay.
- calorie_goal: integer, muc tieu calo tai ngay do.
- created_at: timestamptz.
- updated_at: timestamptz.

Rang buoc:
- Moi user chi co 1 summary cho 1 ngay.
- Unique: user_id + summary_date.

profiles

4. STORAGE BUCKET CAN TAO

4.1. meal-images
- Luu anh mon an nguoi dung chup/chon.
- Path goi y: user_id/year/month/file_name.jpg.
- Vi du: 3f9.../2026/05/meal_123.jpg.

4.2. avatars
- Luu anh dai dien.
- Path goi y: user_id/avatar.jpg.


5. LUONG DU LIEU CHINH

5.1. Dang ky
- Supabase Auth tao user trong auth.users.
- Trigger tu dong tao dong profiles voi id = auth.users.id.
- daily_calorie_goal mac dinh co the la 2000, sau do user vao Setting de sua.

5.2. Cai dat muc tieu calo
- User vao Profile/Setting.
- Nhap muc tieu calo moi ngay.
- App update profiles.daily_calorie_goal.

5.3. Chup anh va AI nhan dien
- User chup/chon anh.
- App upload anh len bucket meal-images.
- App gui anh cho AI.
- Luu ket qua vao meal_ai_results.
- Hien thi ten mon va calo du doan cho user xem/sua.

5.4. Them vao lich su
- Khi user bam Them vao lich su:
  + Tao dong moi trong meals.
  + Cap nhat meal_ai_results.meal_id neu co.
  + Tu dong cap nhat daily_summaries cua ngay do.

5.5. Sua/xoa bua an
- Cap nhat hoac xoa dong trong meals.
- Tu dong tinh lai daily_summaries cua ngay bi anh huong.


6. INDEX CAN CO

- profiles.id.
- meals.user_id + meal_date.
- meals.user_id + eaten_at.
- meal_ai_results.user_id.
- meal_ai_results.meal_id.
- daily_summaries.user_id + summary_date.


7. BAO MAT DU LIEU RLS

Nen bat Row Level Security cho cac bang:
- profiles.
- meals.
- meal_ai_results.
- daily_summaries.

Nguyen tac:
- User chi duoc xem/sua profile co id = auth.uid().
- User chi duoc xem/sua/xoa du lieu co user_id = auth.uid().
- Khong user nao doc duoc lich su calo cua user khac.


8. CAC QUERY APP CAN DUNG

8.1. Lay profile hien tai
- Select tu profiles where id = auth.uid().

8.2. Cap nhat muc tieu calo moi ngay
- Update profiles set daily_calorie_goal = gia_tri_moi where id = auth.uid().

8.3. Lay danh sach mon an trong ngay
- Select tu meals where user_id = auth.uid() and meal_date = ngay_can_xem order by eaten_at desc.

8.4. Lay thong ke 7 ngay gan nhat
- Select tu daily_summaries where user_id = auth.uid() and summary_date between start_date and end_date order by summary_date.

8.5. Them mon an moi
- Insert vao meals.
- Trigger tu dong tinh lai daily_summaries.

8.6. Sua mon an
- Update meals.
- Trigger tu dong tinh lai daily_summaries.

8.7. Xoa mon an
- Delete meals.
- Trigger tu dong tinh lai daily_summaries.
